Learning guitar can be frustrating but incredibly rewarding. If you are having trouble, then here you will find some tips to help you learn.

Playing will not be the same for everyone. The things you want to focus on will differ depending on the genre.

Do you want to be able to shred the guitar? Learn fingerstyle for acoustic playing? Whatever your choice, you will need to learn the basics like chords, scales and building finger strength.
